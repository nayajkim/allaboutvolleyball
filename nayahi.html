what is volleyball and why is it fun?

volleyball is a team sport consisting of 12-15 players. to start a rally, a player serves over the net and the other team receives the ball.
if the ball is received, the team will set the ball and spike it over to get a point.

  • you can play anywhere! grass, sand, or a normal court :>
  • you can play offense and defense
  • its a great team sport

volleyball positions

volleyball has 6 positions. a middle blocker, two opposite hitters on the left and right, a setter,
a libero, and two defense specialists. a setter will always get second ball after it is received by either
a DS or libero. a setter will then set an opposite hitter or a middle. if the ball is received on the opposite side,
two or three blockers will go up and try to block the ball.

volleyball terms

  • ace - when a player serves the ball and the other team is unable to return it.
  • kill - when a player spikes the ball and the other team is unable to return it.
  • side out - when a ball goes out of the court.
  • dig - when someone dives for a ball.
  • stuff - when the defensive player blocks a ball so hard that it is immediately returned to the hitter
    and goes straight to the floor.
